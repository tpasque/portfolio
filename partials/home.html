About me

Prior to the full stack program at Galvanize I attended the University of Colorado where I received my engineering degree as well as a minor in economics. While at CU, I played on the CU Men's Rugby team as well as the Collegiate All-American Men's Rugby team.

After college I worked for a winery in a sales/management/marketing program. Shortly following the wine world, I worked at Iconergy, a start up energy engineering firm. While working at the Iconergy, I invested and started CrossFit Colfax which is located in Denver Colorado as well as a start up tech project that is still under way. I am recently married and live in Denver Colorado.

Professionally, I enjoy start up activity and the process of building something towards a common goal. I have worked at both large, medium and small companies and enjoy different aspects to each. My goal is to contribute to the technical effort at a company and if possible, I would like to contribute to the operations as well if needed."
